Challenge Title: Convert Minutes into Seconds

Write a Function that takes an Integer Minutes and Converts it to Seconds.

For Assistance:

1 Minute = 60 Seconds <- Minutes to Seconds

1 Hour = 3600 Seconds ( 60 * 60 ) <- Hours to Seconds

1 Day = 86400 Second ( 24 * 3600 ) <- Days to Seconds

Notes:
